What are the Side Effects?

While it is easy to get started taking prescription medications, it is a struggle to get practical, useful information about the effectiveness, risks, and value of your medications.

Your doctor is probably best positioned to distill this information, but doctor visits are often rushed, and carefully reviewing medications is sometimes not possible.

More often than not, your doctor is trying to convince you to take more medication, rather than help you understand the need for the medications you take.

Can't read the fine print?

Neither the fine-print package inserts that come with your medications, nor the fast-talking drug commercials on TV will help you understand the true risks and benefits of your medication.

Similarly, most online resources also list exhaustive potential side effects and interactions without actually telling you which problems you should really worry about.

Our Solution

GradeMyMeds.com provides a better alternative, particularly if you are taking multiple medications. Created by an experienced medical team, GMM analyzes and "grades" a single medicine or your entire medication list, distilling the most important side effects, drug interactions, warnings and practical tips in a medication “report card” that is easy to read and understand.

With ratings for Effectiveness, Risk, and Value, we help prepare you to work with your doctor to reduce medications, spend less, and better understand the medications that you are taking.

The information in your report is personalized, valuable, and for most, can lead to better health and savings of hundreds of dollars.
